Full description

***IMMACULATE & GENEROUS CORNER PLOT*** William's Way Estate Agents are proud to present this 3 bedroom semi-detached house on the outskirts of Norwich City Centre. The property sits on a generous corner plot featuring off-road parking with rear and side gardens. The interior is finished to a high standard and is ready to move straight into. The property also benefits from a living/dining room, good size kitchen, three bedrooms, bathroom, garage, UPVC double glazed windows and gas central heating. Early viewings are highly recommended for this sought after property.
